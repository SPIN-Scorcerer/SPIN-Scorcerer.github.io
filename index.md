## SPIN-Scorcerer code repository
<img align="left" src="https://SPIN-Scorcerer.github.io/256px-Magic_Hat.svg.png" alt="SPIN-Scorcerer Logo, a sorcerer's or wizard's pointed blue hat with yellow stars on it" style="margin: 5px 15px 5px 0px; height: 80px; width: 80px;">This page provides an index of SPIN-Scorcerer code, authored by Meredith Riggs in the SoundBrain Lab at the University of Texas (Austin). The software uses common NLP tools (e.g., Python's Pattern module) to analyze human-transcribed responses to speech in noise perception tasks. Please contact the development team for questions or assistance.
